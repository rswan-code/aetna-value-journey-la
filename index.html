How this model works
"It is not the outreach that uncovers barriers — it is sustained engagement over time."
  • Onboarding leads with member needs first — before app download, before registration. Trust is built at first contact.
  • The care team is staffed for depth: appointment scheduling, barrier navigation, and care coordination — not just check-ins.
  • More calls and longer calls are the mechanism. 26 minutes per call is enough time to surface what a 14-minute call misses entirely.
Note on April rate: Engagement rate moderated in April as the program manages pace relative to the contract period. This reflects deliberate pacing — not a change in program effectiveness. The engagement built in prior months is converting into outcomes now.

At the Current Inflection Point

Three paths forward.

  • Allows engagement already built to fully compound into outcomes over the remainder of the contract period
  • Largest potential return — full population reach with the engagement depth driving results

Pyx Advises Against

  • Reaches same members with fewer or shorter interactions
  • Our concern: the 31% prenatal/postpartum rate is a product of depth, not reach — reducing intensity limits the mechanism that produces it
  • Likely to reduce care outcome rates in subsequent reporting periods
